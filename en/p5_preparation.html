Arctic sea cucumber internal meat and tendons(red part) are very rich in nutriments; do not throw them away. To give the greatest value to our client, thehead, tail, teeth, organs have been removed during the process. Only the essence of the arctic sea cucumber has been kept: the meat and tendons, amongst with the skin.

Methon#1: Soak dried sea cucumbers for two days in the refrigerator ( change water once per day ); put in clean clod water in a pot, bring to boil and simmer for 60 minutes; let sea cucumbers cool down naturally; then place them in the refrigerator and soak for 2-3days ( change water once per day ) and the sea cucumbers are ready.

Methon#2: Put the sea cucumbers in clean cold water in a pot,bring to boil and simmer for 3 minutes, then let it cool down naturally; change the water and repeat the last step; change the water, bring to boil and simmer for 30-45minutes, then let it cool down naturally and sea cucumbers are ready.
